Short Biography

Welcome! I am a PhD graduated from University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, advised by Professor Zhi-Li Zhang. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in software engineering and computer science from Southeast University in 2011 and 2013 respectively. I started my first full-time job in streaming team (samza, kafka, and brooklyn) at LinkedIn! My research interests are Computer Networks and Distributed Systems. I published 12 papers in these fields, and very luckily one of my papers was awarded as best paper. In addition, I received software designing competition awards and published system-oriented papers in my bachelor years. Moreover, I interned in both research labs and enterprises, and filed 5 patents during internships. The internship experiences help me a lot in understanding what kind of working environment fits me most. During internships at different research labs and companies, my diligence and initiative were appreciated by my mentors and managers. In my spare time, I enjoy crayon painting. It is a pleasure to show my paintings to my friends, and I was told that they like my paintings a lot.
